Introduction: Makoto Shinkai

Since the release of Kimi No Nawa (2016), one of the highest grossing anime-film at the time release, Makoto Shinkai (新海 誠) is at the fore-front of rising anime directors. Due to his stunning achievement, many have nicknamed him, “The New Hayao Miyazaki”, the creator behind the previous highest-grossing anime-film, Spirited Away (2001).

Works

Makoto Shinkai has produced various works, from short movies to feature-length movies. One of his well-known first work: “She and Her Cat” (1999), was a 5-minute animation piece. As he continue his career, he continue directing new movies, some of them are: “5-Centimeters Per Second”(2007), “The Garden of Worlds” (2013), “your name.” (2016) and recently “Weathering With You” (2019).

Awards

Throughout the years, Makoto Shinkai’s works have recevied various awards, Japan Academy Film Prize for Screenplay of the Year (2016) and Japan Academy Prize for Popularity Award (2016) solely from “your name.” His other works such as “The Garden of World” received iTunes Best of 2013 Best Animation Selection and Best Animated Feature Film in Germany’s 21st Stuttgart Festival of Animated Film (2013).
