INTRODUCTION

Connecting bridges is a processing (Java) assignment for Trinity College IDM Masters course. This assignment was initially a way for us to show the lecturers the things we learning using Processing (Java) but ended become my experiment in coding games.


Connecting Bridges, is a game where you solve “stepping puzzles” to collect clues.


Collecting all clues and returning to the hub will allow you to see the ending. The story is about a person who is trying to reconnect with her old friends but is scared. Luckily, she noticed that her friend also has trinkets of series and games she also liked, these trinkets are the clues you collect in game.


The experimentation of this game is the ability of people to create their custom “stepping puzzles”. Make a CSV file and the processing game will generate the level with it.
